Our team members–the “influencers”–are students from top universities in the United States. Together, we develop innovative solutions grounded in marketing principles and data analytics.

Michelle Yang

Michelle co-founded IA last summer due to her passion for bettering the global community. On campus, Michelle is the Chief Research Officer of Smart Woman Securities and an Asset and Wealth Management Analyst for Cornell’s Society of Women in Business. As an avid reader and writer, Michelle is also a business writing consultant for her school’s Communication Center. In her free time, Michelle enjoys binging The Office, cooking squid ink pasta, and lumbering after her four baby turtles.
